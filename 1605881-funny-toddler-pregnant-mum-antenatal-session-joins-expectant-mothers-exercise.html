Funny Toddler Follows Pregnant Mum to Antenatal Session, Joins Expectant Mothers to Exercise

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  383 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
  • A pregnant Nigerian woman shared a hilarious video after bringing her toddler along to her antenatal appointment
  • In the trending clip, the funny girl joined pregnant women at the hospital to do their exercises that aid labour
  • Social media users who came across the video were left in stitches as they commented on the toddler's action

A playful toddler stole the show during an antenatal session as she joined expectant mothers to exercise themselves.

The little girl's mother, who was attending the session, brought her along and recorded the funny encounter.

Little girl exercises with pregnant women

The mum, identified as @that_kalabarigirl on TikTok, shared the entertaining video, showcasing her child's eagerness to join in on the exercises designed to aid labour.
