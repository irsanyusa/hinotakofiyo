20+ Actors and Their Stunt Doubles Who Are Like Two Peas in a Pod

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Aldo Pusey

We never notice whether it’s the actual actor who’s doing the stunts, or if it’s their stunt double when we watch a movie. 2 completely unrelated people can be made to look like siblings, and we can’t even see a difference. Sometimes stunt performers look similar to their counterparts even without significantly altering their appearance!

Bright Side has found some cool photos of actors and their stunt doubles that look like 2 peas in a pod. There’s also a funny bonus video that awaits you at the end of the article!
