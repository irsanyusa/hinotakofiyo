Dad Becomes Barbie and Takes Daughter to Watch the Movie (More Photos Inside)

 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Aldo Pusey

In a touching and joyful gesture, Eleazar Rodriguez made his daughter, Katherine, incredibly happy by taking her to watch the movie Barbie. But what made this outing truly special was that Eleazar dressed up as Barbie himself, wearing a tutu and embracing the character. This act of love showed how much parents would do to bring joy to their children.

Katherine asked her dad to wear something pink to the premier.

Eleazar’s love for his daughter knows no bounds. When she asked him with a playful challenge, “Daddy, take me to the Barbie movie premiere, but wear something pink, or will you be embarrassed?” he wholeheartedly embraced the idea, knowing it would delight her. In an interview, Eleazar humbly shared, “For the sake of pleasing my family, I would do anything.”

Eleazar’s wife, Alejandra Ortiz, played a crucial role in making his special gesture possible. With Katherine’s tutus as their only option, Alejandra happily assisted in crafting a tutu for Eleazar. As he excitedly explained, “We had already purchased the movie premiere tickets online a month before. The idea of the tutu was spontaneous.” Alejandra’s support and creativity made it all come together, ensuring Eleazar could fully embrace the Barbie persona and make their daughter’s dream a reality.

They had a great time.

The family had so much fun together at the Barbie movie premiere. Though Eleazar was initially hesitant about wearing the tutu, his daughter’s smile and her holding his hand with pride gave him the confidence to not care about what others might think. As they arrived at the cinema, all eyes were on him — people asking for photos, and some even pointing and admiring his bravery.

Despite the attention, Eleazar found it all immensely enjoyable. He shared, “From the moment I stepped out of my truck in the cinema parking lot, people started congratulating me, which gave me even more courage because, honestly, I was a little embarrassed.”

The family never thought that they would go viral.

The family never anticipated their simple outing to the cinema would turn into a viral sensation. Eleazar clarified, “I want to make it clear that I wore the tutu to please my daughter. I am truly surprised by how far the photos have spread, as I never did it with the intention of seeking attention on social media; it was just a family thing that got out of hand.”

Despite the unexpected attention, he remained steadfast in his love for his daughter. Responding to criticism for wearing a tutu, Eleazar courageously stated, “Thank you to everyone for your comments; there are more positive ones. Believe me, wearing a tutu and dressing in pink doesn’t make you any less of a man. I have the courage to fulfill my daughter’s wishes, and I’d go to great lengths for her happiness.”
