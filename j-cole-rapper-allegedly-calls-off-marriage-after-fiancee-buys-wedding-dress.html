Rapper allegedly calls off marriage after fiancee buys wedding dress

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  264 words  |  Martina Birk

Cole proposed to his longtime girlfriend and wedding planner Melissa Heholt over five years ago but the uncertainty of the date of their wedding has become a topic on the tabloids for months now.

Sister 2 Sister reported that Cole proposed to Melissa since December 2010 and  the couple had picked a date and location for their wedding. The bride-to-be was spotted at a bridal shop in New York in January which may have rested all doubts about the wedding.

The wedding vendor then shared an image of Heholt on their Instagram with a caption: "Congratulations to our newest bride #weddinggown #weddingdress #lfaybridal #nycbride call us for your appointment in our new location."

"I feel sorry for Melissa because he is still denying that he's engaged. He must think everyone is as stupid as she is. This b***h must really be pregnant too. I can't believe I was stupid enough to believe this a**hole."

Adding that, "And he says you're boring in bed sweetie. He talks about how dry you are thats how he describes you. Everything about you is dry as f**k, his words not mine. You two deserve each other," the lady lashed out in a recent interview with the site.
