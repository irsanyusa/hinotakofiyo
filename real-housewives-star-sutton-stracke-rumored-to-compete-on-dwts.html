Dancing with the Stars Season 33 isn’t expected to premiere until this fall, but the casting rumors have already begun. According to a new report, Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Sutton Stracke could be one of the celebrities competing in the upcoming season.

RHOBH Star Sutton Stracke Rumored to Be on DWTS

According to Taste of Reality, the gossip account Deux Moi recently shared a tip from an anonymous source claiming that a “very fancy” star of RHOBH “who wasn’t fond of Magic Mike’s dancing has been signed for DWTS.”

Fans have pieced together that the clues point to Stracke, who happens to be on the board of governing trustees for the American Ballet Theater. Commenters on both the RHOBH and DWTS Reddit pages shared their thoughts on the potential casting.

“I’d love to see it in theory.. but no.. I actually can’t picture this at all,” wrote one user on the RHOBH Reddit. Another shared, “She seems too fragile to be on this show.” Another user pointed out that Housewives usually don’t go far on the show.

“Love Sutton but this is going to be terrible,” wrote a user on the DWTS Reddit. Someone else thought Stracke would be “interesting,” pointing out that she’s “very awkward but also was a ballerina.”

Bravo Star Ariana Madix Made It to the Finale

Taste of Reality points out that eight Real Housewives stars have competed on DWTS over the years, including Teresa Giudice, Lisa Vanderpump, and Nene Leakes. Lisa Rinna made it the farthest, placing fourth in Season 2.

Although not a Housewife, Bravo star Ariana Madix also had an impressive showing during last year’s competition. The Vanderpump Rules cast member made it all the way to the finale, finishing the show in third place.
