In a surprising turn of events, the teen drama series Chicken Girls will welcome some new faces in its upcoming season. The announcement has sparked a wave of excitement among fans, as it has been revealed that the three remaining members of the popular girl group XOMG POP! will be making their way to the world of Attaway High.

XOMG POP! Girls to Star in Upcoming Chicken Girls Season

Brooklynn McKnight, Dallas Skye, and Tinie T, the trio that makes up the current lineup of XOMG POP!, have been featured in the promotional trailer for the upcoming season of Chicken Girls. This unexpected crossover has already set social media abuzz with speculation and anticipation.

Chicken Girls, known for its relatable portrayal of high school experiences and friendships, has been a staple among teen viewers since its debut. With its engaging storylines and vibrant characters, the series has certainly managed to capture the essence of teenage life, making it a perfect platform for the young talents of XOMG POP! to showcase their acting skills.

The inclusion of Brooklynn, Dallas, and Tinie in the new season hints at exciting plot developments. While details about their roles remain under wraps, fans are already speculating about potential story arcs and interactions with the show’s existing characters.

This crossover marks a significant step for XOMG POP! as they expand their horizons beyond the music world. It also offers a unique opportunity for Chicken Girls to infuse its narrative with the energy and charm of XOMG POP!

XOMG POP! is Looking For New Members

As the anticipation builds for Season 11 of Chicken Girls featuring the remaining members of XOMG POP!, the group itself has also announced that it’s on the hunt for fresh talent to join its ranks. With their recent Instagram post urging aspiring talents to seize the chance to become the next XOMG POP! sensation, the group is clearly gearing up for an exciting new chapter.

To submit your audition, all you need to do is click on the link in their bio, which will take you to the official audition portal. There, you can showcase your singing, dancing, and overall star quality. It’s an opportunity to then step into the spotlight and potentially become the next member of this dynamic and vibrant group.

With the departure of Kiya Barczyszyn, Leigha Sanderson, Kinley Cunningham, and Bella Cianni Llerena in the past year, the group has both evolved and adapted. These former members, once integral to the ensemble, have now paved the way for a new era of XOMG POP!.
